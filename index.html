I am a Ph.D. student at the Institute for International Economic Studies (IIES) at Stockholm University.

I hold a B.Sc. and a M.Sc. in Economic and Social Sciences from Bocconi University and before moving to Stockholm I worked at Bocconi University as research assistant and at the European Central Bank.

My primary field is macroeconomics, with a focus on income risk and its consequences for agents’ choices and the macroeconomy.
